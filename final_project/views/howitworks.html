How SkillMatch Works

Connecting students with mentors to grow your skills and achieve your goals!

1

Create a Profile

Sign up on SkillMatch by creating a profile. Share your interests, skills, and goals to connect with the right mentors.

2

Set Your Learning Goals

Define what you want to achieve. Whether it's academic growth, career advice, or personal development, SkillMatch helps you find mentors who can guide you.

3

Get Matched with Mentors

Once your profile is complete, SkillMatch uses your interests and goals to match you with mentors (teachers or senior students) who can help you grow in the areas you care about.

4

Start Learning and Growing

Engage in meaningful conversations with your mentors. Receive guidance, ask questions, and work towards achieving your learning goals!
